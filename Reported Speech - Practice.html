Exercise 2

You will find 10 sentences in indirect speech. Choose the correct direct speech sentence.

7: Jane said that she was enjoying the weather.

'I was enjoying the weather,' said Jane.
'I am enjoying the weather,' said Jane.

8: He mentioned that he had bought a new car.

'I have bought a new car,' he mentioned.
'I will buy a new car,' he mentioned.

9: She asked if they wanted to join her for coffee.

'Did you want to join me for coffee?' she asked.
'Do you want to join me for coffee?' she asked.

10: They said that they hadn't travelled abroad before.

'We hadn't travelled abroad before,' they said.
'We haven't travelled abroad before,' they said.

11: He asked whether I could complete the report by Friday.

'Can you be completing the report by Friday?' he asked.
'Can you complete the report by Friday?' he asked.

12: She said that she was surprised by the news.

'I was surprised by the news,' she said.
'I am surprised by the news,' she said.

13: He stated that he couldn't attend the meeting.

'I can't attend the meeting,' he stated.
'I can't be attending the meeting,' he stated.

14: She asked if I knew where her bag was.

'Did you know where my bag is?' she asked.
'Do you know where my bag is?' she asked.
