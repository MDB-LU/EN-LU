Past Simple - Questions

If we want to make questions in the Past Simple, we have to use an auxiliary verb. Most of the time this auxiliary verb is did.

Examples:

Did you sleep well?

Did you go to school in England?

Did you play football last weekend?


We use the auxiliary verb did in every person.

Examples:

Did I work yesterday?

Did you work yesterday?

Did he work yesterday? / Did she work yesterday? / Did it work yesterday?

Did we work yesterday?

Did you work yesterday?

Did they work yesterday?


IMPORTANT

When we use the auxiliary verb did, we have to use the base form (infinitive) of the main verb.

Examples:

Did you play football last weekend? NOT: Did you played football last weekend?

Did she catch the ball? NOT: Did she caught the ball?


Quick Check

Select the correct question.

1.

Did he move to Los Angeles? Did he moves to Los Angeles?

2.

Did you like the concert yesterday? Do you like the concert yesterday?

3.

Where did you go for your last vacation? Where did you went for your last vacation?

4.

What did they had for breakfast this morning? What did they have for breakfast this morning?
