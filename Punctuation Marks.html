Punctuation Marks

If you are working with a screenreader like Fusion, it is very important to know the punctuation marks. Of course you already know what they are called in German, but if you are reading an English text or email, you should also know what they are called in English.

Below, you will find a list of the most common punctuation marks in English:


. is called dot, period or full stop

, is called comma

: is called colon

; is called semicolon

? is called question mark

! is called exclamation mark

- is called dash

_ is called underline

' is called apostrophe

/ is called slash

( is called left parenthesis

) is called right parenthesis

[ is called left bracket

] is called right bracket
