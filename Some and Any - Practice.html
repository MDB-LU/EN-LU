Exercise 1

Choose which sentence is correct and incorrect.

1:

There aren't some apples.

There aren't any apples.

2:

Do you know some nice place to eat?

Do you know a nice place to eat?

3:

I have some pictures of the beach.

I have any pictures of the beach.

4:

Could I have some juice, please?

Could I have any juice, please?

5:

There are some chairs in the living room.

There are any chairs in the living room.
