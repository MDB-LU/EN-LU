Exercise 3

Choose the sentence where the adverb is in the correct place.

13: never

He never forgets to lock the door when he leaves the house.
He forgets never to lock the door when he leaves the house.

14: slowly

The slowly snail moved across the road.
The snail slowly moved across the road.

15: sometimes

They go to the beach when the weather is warm sometimes.
They sometimes go to the beach when the weather is warm.

16: rarely

I rarely drink soda because I prefer water.
I drink soda because I prefer water rarely.

17: almost

She almost finished her homework before dinner.
She finished her almost homework before dinner.

18: carefully

She opened carefully the box.
She opened the box carefully.
