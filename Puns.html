Puns

A pun is a humorous use of a word in such a way as to suggest two or more of its meanings or the meaning of another word similar in sound.

Task

Read the following puns below and choose at least three.

Learn the three puns by heart and say them aloud. Practice them to get the delivery correct.

Once you feel ready to record the puns, you can follow the link at the end of this page.
