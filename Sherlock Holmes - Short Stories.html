Sherlock Holmes - Short Stories

Sherlock Holmes is one of the most famous detectives in literary history. In this lesson, you will find three short stories along with some exercises.

Before you start reading, let's find out how much you know about Shelock Holmes.


Quiz:

Choose the correct option for the following questions:


1. What is the name of Sherlock Holmes' partner?

Professor Turner Doctor Watson Mister Magoo

2. What is Sherlock Holmes' address:

221B Baker Street in London 112A Brooklyn Boulevard in New York 121C Fifth Avenue in Liverpool

3. Who wrote the books and stories about Sherlock Holmes?

Stephen King William Shakespeare Arthur Conan Doyle

Solutions:

Sherlock Holmes lives in London at 221B Baker Street. He often works with his partner Doctor Watson. Ther stories are written through Doctor Watson's perspective. The real writer of the books is Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.
