Word Checker

On this page, you can play a game called Word Checker.

You can choose between several versions. You will be given six letters. How many different words can you make out of these six letters?

Example: You have the letters S T O N E D. Out of these six letters, you can form the words net, one, stone and many more.

A letter can only be used once.

Choose one of the links below and see, how many words you can form. If you are curious to find out, whether you have missed some words or not, you can download the wordlist with all the words and the German translation at the bottom of the page.
