The Synonym Game

On this page, you can play a game which is all about Synonyms

This game is all about the different words that can have the same meaning.


As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are three minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have the same question: What is the synonym of ...?

Every question is about a different word. You always have four options. Navigate the page with arrow up and arrow down and choose the correct answer by pressing Enter.

The options will be read to you in English. After each option, you hear the word "Schalter". This stands for Schalter.

You will find out if your answer was wrong or right and then the next question appears. To save time, I suggest pressing Control + Home to go to the beginning of the page, so you can hear the sentence.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
