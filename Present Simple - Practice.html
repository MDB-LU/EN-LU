Present Simple - Practice

Complete the following exercises. At the end of the page, you will find a button called Check Answers. Click this button and you will find out your score and your mistakes.


Exercise 1

Choose the correct form of the verb in the Present Simple:

1: Sarah always to school in the morning.

2: My father the newspaper every day.

3: The bus at the corner near my house.

4: Birds beautifully at sunrise.

5: We breakfast together every weekend.


Exercise 2

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in brackets in the Present Simple.

6: He (read) books before going to bed.

7: They (travel) to Italy every summer.

8: She (write) emails at work.

9: It (shine) brightly in the summer.

10: I (need) a cup of coffee in the morning.


Exercise 3

Complete the questions in the Present Simple:

11: they enjoy swimming?

12: Does he a car?

13: you visit your grandparents often?

14: she eat lunch at home?

15: Does the teacher homework every day?

16: it take long to get there?


Exercise 4

Make negative sentences. Do NOT use contractions. For example: Write do not and NOT don't.

17: She (not / read) novels.

18: They (not / eat) fast food.

19: He (not / work) in a hospital.

20: We (not / drive) to the office.

21: It (not / happen) often.
