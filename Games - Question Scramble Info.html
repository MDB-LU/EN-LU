Question Scramble

Questions over questions over questions! This is the topic of this game.

As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are five minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have a question, but the words of this question are NOT in the correct order.

Below the words, you have an Eingabefeld where you have to write in the words in the correct order.

IMPORTANT: Start the question with a capital letter and don't forget the question mark at the end.


To write down your answer, you have to activate the Eingabefeld by pressing the space bar (Leertaste). To check your answer, press Enter.

You will find out if your anwer was wrong or right and then the next question appears. If you want to hear the new words, you have to press Escape and then arrow up.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
