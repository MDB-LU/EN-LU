Exercise 1

Choose the sentence where the commas are in the correct place.

1:

She packed sandwiches juice cookies and chips, for the road trip.
She packed sandwiches, juice, cookies and chips for the road trip.

2:

The sports team includes players, coaches, trainers and managers.
The sports team includes players, coaches, trainers, and managers.

3:

For dinner, we had steak, potatoes, salad and dessert.
For dinner we had steak, potatoes, salad and dessert.

4:

He likes painting and listening to music.
He likes painting, and listening to music.

5:

The bookshelf held novels biographies dictionaries and photo albums.
The bookshelf held novels, biographies, dictionaries and photo albums.
