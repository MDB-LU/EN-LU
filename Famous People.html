Famous People

In this section of the Lernumgebung you get a task which you can do on your own.

On this page you will find 10 names of famous people. They are all from different walks of life, for example sports, economy, politics, film, art, etc. They are all from English-speaking countries.


Task

At the end of this course you will have to present two things to your English teacher. Firstly, a written summary in your own words about the famous person you have chosen. Secondly, a short oral presentation, where you tell your teacher about the person in 5 to 10 minutes. The different steps of the course can be found in the Lernumgebung. Just follow the steps.

1. Step

Read the short descriptions of the ten famous people and choose one of them.


Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is the greatest basketball player of all time. He won six NBA Championships with the Chicago Bulls.


Oprah Winfrey

Oprah Winfrey is a talk show host, actress, and philanthropist. Her talk show became the highest-rated television program of its kind in history.


Tom Cruise

Tom Cruise is an actor playing in films like "Top Gun" and "Mission: Impossible". He is known for doing his own stunts in his action films.


Yoko Ono

Yoko Ono is an artist known for her experimental art. Her work is very unconventional and experimental.


Barack Obama

Barack Obama is the first African American President of the United States. He was President from 2009 to 2017.


J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for writing the "Harry Potter" books. She is the one of the world's best selling authors.


Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking was a theoretical physicist. He wrote several books and worked on black holes.


Naomi Campbell

Naomi Campbell is a supermodel. She was on the cover of a lot of magazines and walked the runway for top designers.


Robin Williams

Robin Williams was a comedian and actor known for his improvisational skills. His performances were both hilarious and moving.


Rosa Parks

Rosa Parks was an African American activist known for her important role in the Montgomery Bus Boycott in the 1950s. The Boycott was a significant movement against racial discrimination.
