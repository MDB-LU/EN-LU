Will Future - Practice

Complete the following exercises. At the end of the page, you will find a button called Check Answers. Click this button and you will find out your score and your mistakes.

Exercise 1

Complete the following sentences using the will future.

1: I think she (win) the competition.

2: They (arrive) before midnight.

3: We (visit) our grandparents during the holidays.

4: He (eat) dinner because he is hungry.

5: She (call) you later to discuss the plans.

Exercise 2

Rewrite the following sentences using the negative form of the will future.

IMPORTANT: Do NOT use contractions. Write will not NOT won't.

6: They will come to the meeting.

7: I will clean the house today.

8: She will invite her classmates to the party.

9: We will visit the new museum.

10: He will join the football team next year.

Exercise 3

Rewrite the following statements as questions using the will future.

11: They will start the project next month.

12: He will cook dinner for us.

13: We will attend the conference next week.

14: She will meet her friends at the mall.

15: I will buy a gift for her birthday.

16: He will not tell anyone the secret.
