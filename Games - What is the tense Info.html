What's the tense?

On this page, you can play a game called What's the tense?

This game is all about the different tenses in English, so make sure to have a look at those on the Lernumgebung.

The passive form of the different tenses will be tested as well. If you want to know how we make the passive, then click the following link:

Passives


As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are three minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have a sentence with one verb form. Your task is to decide which tense this verb form is in.

For every question, you have four options. Choose the correct option.

You will find out if your answer was wrong or right and then the next question appears.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
