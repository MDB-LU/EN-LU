The Pronoun Game

On this page, you can play a game called which is all about pronouns

If you want to look at the topic before playing the game, I suggest looking at the pronoun section on the following page:

Parts of Speech


As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are three minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have a sentence with one one gap. Below you will find four options. Every option is a pronoun and you have to choose the correct one.

Navigate the page with arrow up and arrow down and choose the correct answer by pressing Enter.

The options will be read to you in English. After each option, you hear the word "Schalter". This stands for Schalter.

You will find out if your answer was wrong or right and then the next sentence appears. To save time, I suggest pressing Control + Home to go to the beginning of the page, so you can hear the sentence.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
