Solutions to Exercise 1

1: False. Sherlock Holmes never loved a woman.

2: True.

3: True.

4: Irene doesn't want the King to marry another woman.

5: The King just said: 'Money is not important.'

6: True.

7: Three taxis drove to the church.

8: True.

Solutions to Exercise 2

9: Who fell, with blood running down his face?

Sherlock Holmes, dressed as a servant.

10: Why did Irene bring Holmes into her house?

Because he helped her escape the fight.

11: Why did Watson throw the smoke-stick into the room?

Because Irene thought there was a fire. And when there is a fire, a woman runs to the most important thing in her house. Her photograph.

12: What happened next?

They went to the house of Irene the next day. There, they find out, that Irene and her husband have left the county.

13: Where did Irene hide the photo?

In a cupboard in the sitting-room.

14: Who said, 'Good night, Mr Sherlock Holmes'?

Irene, dressed as a young man.

15: Why was the King happy in the end?

Because he needs nothing more than Irene’s word.

16: What did Holmes ask the King to give him?

The photograph of Irene which was left in the cupboard. Sherlock Holmes still has the photograph because she was more clever than he was.
