Superheroes

Do you like superheroes? If your answer is yes, this is the right page for you on the Lernumgebung.

You will get a task which you can do on your own and it's all about superheroes.

Read the task below to find out what you have to do.


Task

At the end of this course you will have to present two things to your English teacher. Firstly, a filled out worksheet where you find things on the Internet about three different superheroes. Secondly, an written text in which you tell a story about a superhero that you have created yourself.

The different steps of the course can be found in the Lernumgebung. Just follow the steps.


1. Step

To begin this course, you can do the following quiz to see how much you really now about superheroes.

Select the correct answers. If you have answered all the questions and press on the button Check Answers, it will tell you how many question you have answered correctly. Remember that number!

1. Which superhero has a big S on his chest?



2. Which superhero can shoot webs from his wrists?



3. Which superhero often fights against the Joker?



4. Which of these superheroes has a shield?



5. Which of these superheroes comes from the island Themyscira?



6. Superheroes can also be part of a group or work in a team. Which of the following is a name of a group of Superheroes?
