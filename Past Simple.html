Past Simple

We use the Past Simple to describe events that happened in the past. These events are finished.

Examples:

I worked in a bank.

We played football.

They stayed in a hotel.


Regular Verbs

In the Past Simple, we have regular and irregular verb forms. In this chapter, we will focus on the regular verb forms. Regular verb forms all have the same ending: -ed.

Examples:

I watched television.

We played football on Saturday.

They stayed in a hotel last weekend.


For verbs that already end with an -e, we just have to add a -d.

Examples:

hate = hated

believe = believed


The verb form in the Past Simple stays the same for every person.

Examples:

I worked

You worked

He worked / she worked / it worked

We worked

You worked

They worked
