Dublin

Dublin is the capital city of Ireland.


Population

592,713 people


Young People

Dublin has the youngest population in all of Europe. Approximately one third of the population are under 25-years of age.


Name

The name Dublin comes from an Old Irish Gaelic phrase 'Dubh linn' that translates to 'Black Pool'.


Guinness

Guinness is brewed in Ireland, and will continue be for many years to come. You may already know that Guinness is brewed in the renowned Guinness Brewery at St James’s Gate while it’s headquarters The Guinness storehouse is also located there and is one of the cities major tourist attractions. But did you know that company holds a one of a kind 9000-year lease for the property which does not expire until 10759.
