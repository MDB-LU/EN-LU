Sentence Scramble

As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are five minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have a sentence, but the words of this sentence are NOT in the correct order.

Below the words, you have an Eingabefeld where you have to write in the words in the correct order.

IMPORTANT: Start the sentence with a capital letter and don't forget the period at the end.


To check your answer, press Enter on the keyboard.

You will find out if your anwer was wrong or right and then the next question appears.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
