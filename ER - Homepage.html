Escape Room

You are locked into your English classroom. There is only one way to get out of the room, but the door is locked with a combination lock. This lock can only be unlocked if you find out the secret code. This code consists of three single digit numbers.

Follow the instructions on this page and solve the riddles to find out the correct combination. Everything you need can be found in the Lernumgebung. You will need to take notes and you can use and do everything you want to solve the riddles.
