Parts of Speech Game

On this page, you can play the Parts of Speech Game.

As soon as you click on the link below, a timer will start. There are three minutes on the clock.

At the top of the page, you always have a sentence followed by a question. The question is always the same: What part of speech is ...

For every question, you have four options. You will find out if your answer was wrong or right and then the next question appears.

You will be informed once the time is up. After that, you can see how many questions you answered correctly.
