The Adventures of Tom Sawyer - While Reading

On this page, you will find some exercises for the different chapters of The Adventures of Tom Sawyer.


Chapter 1

If you have read chapter 1, you can do this exercise.

Are these sentences true or false?

1. Tom lived with his mother and father.


2. Tom painted all the fence.


3. Huck Finn went to school.


4. Huck wanted to go and see ghosts.


5. Tom wanted to sit with the girls.


6. Becky Thatcher liked Tom's pictures.





Now, read chapter 2.


Chapter 2

If you have read chapter 2, you can do the following two exercises.


Exercise 1

Below, you will read some statements from the book. Select the person who has said these words.

Oh, let's go home.


What are they doing here?


I gave you the money this morning.


I want that money!


Don't hit my friend.


Why did you kill him?


I'm afraid of Injun Joe.





Exercise 2

Before you read chapter 3, can you guess what happens next? Select one option








Now, read chapter 3.
