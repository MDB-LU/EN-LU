Will Future

We use the will future to indicate that something will happen in the future.

To make the future, we have to use the word will and the base form (infinitive) of the main verb.

Examples:

Tanja will win the game.

I will go to bed.

The sun will shine tomorrow.


Negative

If we want to make a negative sentence in the future, we have to add the word not after will and before the main verb.

We can also use the abbreviation won't which stands for will not.

Examples:

I will not be there tomorrow.

He won't go to Paris again.

We will not win this game.


Questions

We can also make questions in the will future. We just have to put will before the subject.

Examples:

Will they be there tomorrow?

Where will they go?

Will Michael marry Tamara?


IMPORTANT

We use the will future to talk about events that cannot be influenced.

Example: The sun will shine tomorrow.

We also use the will future to talk about spontaneous decisions.

Example:

A: I don't have any money.

B: Don't worry, I will pay for you.

Finally, we use the will future to talk about things that we are not sure about that they will actually happen.

Example:

Maybe I will celebrate my birthday this year.
