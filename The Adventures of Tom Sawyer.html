The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

The Adventures of Tom Sawyer is a novel written by Mark Twain, published in 1876, that centres on a smart mischievous young boy living in a town along the Mississippi River. The work is considered a classic of American literature.

This version of the text is intended for people who consider themselves beginners in English.
