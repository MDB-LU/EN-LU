Present Perfect - Practice

Complete the following exercises. At the end of the page, you will find a button called Check Answers. Click this button and you will find out your score and your mistakes.

Exercise 1

Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the present perfect tense:

1: I (complete) my science project this week.

2: She (never/try) rock climbing before.

3: We (visit) that art gallery twice.

4: They (not/clean) their room yet.

5: I (write) three essays this semester.

6: She (never/ride) a horse before.

7: We (already/prepare) for the exam.

8: They (not/finish) their lunch yet.

Exercise 2

11: We already seen that play.

13: I joined the photography club.

14: She cooked dinner for her family.

15: We never been to this park before.

16: the kids started their lessons?

Exercise 3

18: (you/complete) the book I lent you?

19: (they/visit) the new amusement park yet?

20: (we/finish) all the preparations?
