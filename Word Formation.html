Word Formation

On this page, you can play a game called Word Formation.

You will be given a word. How many other words can you form out of this word?

Example: You have the word choose. Out of this word, you can form the words choice, chose, chosen, chooses and more.

Choose one of the words below and see, how many words you can form. If you are curious to find out, whether you have missed some words or not, you can press the button at the end of the page which shows you the solutions.
