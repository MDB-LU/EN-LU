Vocabulary App - Instructions

On these pages, the question will be an English word and the answer will be the German translation.


In order to efficiently use these vocabulary learning sites, you should know the following things:

If you choose a link below, it will lead you to the programme. On there, you will only find an English word with four options below. Navigate to the correct translation and press Enter. It will tell you if your answer was correct or not. For the next question, make sure to press Control + Home and then arrow down to listen to the next word.

If you prefer to have no options, you can click the button Lernmodus wechseln. You will be shown an English word again, but this time you have an Eingabefeld. Before you type in your translation, make sure to press the space bar (Leertaste) to activate the Eingabefeld. If you have written in the German word, press Enter to check if your answer was right or wrong. Press Enter again to close the result, then press Escape to leave the Eingabefeld. Use the arrow up to listen to the next English word, then go down, activate the Eingabefeld and type in your solution. Repeat this step every time. If you click the button Statistik ansehen and go down a few times with arrow down, you can check how many and which words you knew.

Finally, if you think you know the words, you can press the button Testmodus. The programme will generate a test with thirty questions about the vocabulary you have just learned. You can even check the results with a button at the end of the page.

IMPORTANT: Only one word is programmed to be the right answer. For example: The word watch can have multiple translations. It can be the verb schauen or the noun Armbanduhr. But only one of these options will be correct in the list. To find out what the correct translations for the words are, you can download a wordlist for each unit. These lists can be found at the bottom of the pages of the respective sites.
