Tongue Twisters

On this page you can read and learn some tongue twisters.

Task

Read the following tongue twisters below and choose at least one.

Learn the tongue twister by heart and say it aloud. Practice this and try to increase the speed.

Once you feel ready, present your tongue twister to at least two people.


Tongue Twisters

Read the following tongue twisters and choose at least:


1.

A big black bug bit a big black dog on his big black nose.

2.

How much wood would a woodchuck chuck if a woodchuck could chuck wood?

3.

If two witches were watching two watches, which witch would watch which watch?

4.

Not these things here, but those things there.

5.

She sells seashells by the seashore.

6.

He threw three free throws.

7.

A tutor who tooted the flute tried to teach two young tooters to toot. Said the two to the tutor, ‘Is it harder to toot, or to tutor two tooters to toot?

8.

Betty bought a bit of butter. But the butter Betty bought was bitter. So Betty bought a better butter, and it was better than the butter Betty bought before.

9.

I wish to wish the wish you wish to wish, but if you wish the wish the witch wishes, I won’t wish the wish you wish to wish.

10.

Any noise annoys an oyster, but a noisy noise annoys an oyster more.

11.

Which wrist watches are Swiss wrist watches?


Practice

Learn the tongue twister by heart and practice it by saying it out loud. Try to increase the speed.

If you feel ready, present your tongue twister to at least two different people.
