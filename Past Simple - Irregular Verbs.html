Past Simple - Irregular Verbs

In the Past Simple, there are regular and irregular verbs. In this lesson, we will focus on the irregular ones.


Be - Das Verb "sein"

The verb be is one of the most important verbs in English. It is also the only verb in the Past Simple, that has two different forms.

I was

You were

He was / She was / It was

We were

You were

They were


Do

The verb do is another important verb in English. It is often used as an auxiliary verb if we want to make questions or negative sentences.

The Past Simple of do is did. It is the same in every person.


Have

The verb have is the third important verb in English. It can also be used as an auxiliary verb, especially in the Perfect tenses.

The Past Simple of have is had. It is the same in every person.
